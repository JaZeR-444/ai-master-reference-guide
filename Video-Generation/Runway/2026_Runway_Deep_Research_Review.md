## 3. Core Capabilities & Modalities

- **Modality:** Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Video-to-Video, Audio-to-Video.
- **Motion Control:** Multi-Motion Brush and Camera Path Control (industry standard).
- **VFX Tools:** Green screen, inpainting, frame interpolation, and motion tracking.
- **Creative Storyboarding:** Middle Keyframes allow users to define the start, middle, and end of a 10s shot.

---

## 4. Technical Architecture (High-Level)

- **Architecture:** Large-scale latent diffusion models. Runway is increasingly moving toward "World Models" that understand 3D space and physics rather than just pixel patterns.
- **Inference:** High-performance GPU clusters. Offers "Turbo" mode for near-instant previews.
- **Deployment:** SaaS (Web/Mobile) + Enterprise API.

---

## 5. Performance, Quality, and Benchmarks

- **Visual Quality:** Industry-leading. Consistently wins in "photorealism" benchmarks.
- **Motion Accuracy:** High. Best-in-class at maintaining subject identity across 10-20 seconds of video.
- **Resolution:** 1080p native, with in-platform 4K upscaling.

## 6. Pricing, Limits, and Economic Model

Runway uses a credit-based system (1 second = 5-10 credits depending on model).

| Plan | Price (Annual) | Credits/mo | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | $0 | 125 (total) | Watermarked; testing only. |
| **Standard** | ~$12/mo | 625 | 4K Upscaling; remove watermarks. |
| **Pro** | ~$28/mo | 2,250 | All models; priority queue; 500GB storage. |

## 8. Adoption, Ecosystem, and Real-World Usage

- **Adoption:** Used in Oscar-winning films (*Everything Everywhere All At Once*) and by top creative agencies.
- **Ecosystem:** Mobile app is highly rated; API is being adopted by third-party creative apps.

---

## 9. Competitive Landscape

- **Luma Dream Machine:** Strong competitor in cinematic motion; often feels more "fluid" but has fewer control tools.
- **Pika:** Better for stylistic animation and "Pikaffects" (subject transformation).
- **OpenAI Sora:** Technically superior in many ways but has limited public availability and control tools compared to Runway.

---

## 10. Operational Risks & Watch Areas

- **Credit Burn:** High-fidelity generations consume credits rapidly; costs can spike for complex projects.
- **Learning Curve:** Granular controls (Motion Brush) require practice to master.

**Best Fit:** High-end creative work, VFX, and products requiring deep control over motion and characters.
