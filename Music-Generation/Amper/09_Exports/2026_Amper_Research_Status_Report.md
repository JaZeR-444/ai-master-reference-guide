# 2026 Amper Music Research Status Report (Legacy/Integrated)

## 1. Provider Overview & Positioning
Amper Music was one of the first AI music platforms designed for creators to customize music without any technical musical knowledge. 

- **Current Status:** **DEPRECATED / INTEGRATED**. Amper Music was acquired by **Shutterstock** in late 2020.
- **Positioning:** It no longer exists as a standalone consumer platform. Its technology has been folded into Shutterstock's "Creative Flow" and "AI Music" offerings.

## 3. Core Capabilities (Integrated)
- The technology now powers Shutterstock's AI music generation, which allows users to:
  - Select music by mood, genre, and duration.
  - Re-structure tracks to fit video lengths.
  - Access a library of AI-assisted royalty-free tracks.

## 6. Pricing & Access
- Access is now exclusively via **Shutterstock subscriptions**.
- No standalone "Amper" pricing exists in 2026.
