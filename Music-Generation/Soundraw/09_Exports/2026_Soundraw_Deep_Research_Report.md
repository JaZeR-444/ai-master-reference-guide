# 2026 Soundraw Deep Research Report

## 1. Provider Overview & Positioning
Soundraw is an AI music generator designed specifically for creators who need background music but want more control than just a simple library search.

- **Primary Audience:** Video creators, advertisers, and podcasters.
- **Value Proposition:** "Customize the music to fit your video," rather than "edit your video to fit the music."
- **Ecosystem Position:** A productivity tool for the video production workflow.

## 3. Core Capabilities
- **Structural Customization:** Change the length, tempo, and energy of different parts of a song.
- **Instrument Control:** Mute or solo specific instruments in the track.
- **Infinite Generation:** Never-ending variations of a chosen style.
- **Royalty-Free Guarantee:** Clear licensing for all commercial uses.

---

## 4. Technical Architecture
- **Known:** Uses a proprietary AI that generates music in real-time. 
- **Inferred:** Likely uses a rule-based generative system combined with high-quality audio synthesis (WaveNet-style or similar) to ensure the loops transition perfectly.
- **Deployment:** Web-based.

---

## 6. Pricing & Economics (2026)
- **Creator ($11-13/mo):** Unlimited downloads (MP3).
- **Artist Tiers ($20-33/mo):** Includes WAV downloads and Stems.
- **Efficiency:** The "Unlimited Downloads" at the base price makes it one of the best values for high-volume video creators.

---

## 9. Competitive Landscape
- **Vs. Beatoven:** Soundraw offers more "structural" control; Beatoven is more "emotion" focused.
- **Vs. Suno/Udio:** Soundraw is for *instrumental* background use; Suno/Udio are for *songs*.

---

## 11. Strategic Assessment
- **When to invest:** If you are a solo builder/creator producing multiple videos a month and need a reliable, cost-effective source of high-quality background music.
- **When to skip:** If you need songs with lyrics or complex orchestral scores.
- **Confidence Level:** High.
