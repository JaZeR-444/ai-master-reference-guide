## 1. Provider Overview & Positioning

**AIVA (Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist)** is an AI music composition engine specializing in **emotional, cinematic, and classical music**. Unlike many generative music tools that focus on pop or electronic genres, AIVA aims to produce high-quality, orchestral-style soundtracks suitable for film, games, and advertising. It positions itself as a creative assistant for composers and content creators, not just a beat maker.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- **For Builders:** Access to a sophisticated AI composer that can generate royalty-free, original musical scores with customizable emotional and stylistic nuances.
- **For Creatives:** A tool to rapidly ideate, customize, and own orchestral music for professional projects, with full MIDI and WAV export capabilities.

## 3. Core Capabilities & Modalities

*   **AI Music Composition (The Killer Feature):** AIVA composes entirely original pieces of music, rather than remixing existing loops or samples. It learns from a vast database of scores to create new melodies, harmonies, and rhythms.
*   **Genre Specialization:** Uniquely strong in classical and cinematic scores, making it ideal for background music, film scores, and game soundtracks.
*   **Customization Options:** Users can guide the AI by selecting genre, mood, instruments, tempo, and even specifying harmonic complexity or emotional arcs.
*   **MIDI & WAV Export:** For professional users, the ability to export in MIDI format means the AI-generated composition can be further edited and arranged in any Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). High-quality WAV export is available for final production.

## 4. Technical Architecture (High-Level)

*   **Deep Learning Algorithms:** AIVA employs proprietary deep learning algorithms, likely based on recurrent neural networks (RNNs) or transformer architectures, trained on a large corpus of music.
*   **Symbolic AI:** The composition engine operates at a symbolic level (MIDI notes, harmonies, rhythms) before rendering to audio.

## 5. Performance, Quality, and Benchmarks

*   **Musicality:** AIVA's compositions often sound cohesive, emotionally resonant, and structurally sound, particularly in its specialized genres.
*   **Uniqueness:** Generates truly original compositions, avoiding the generic feel of some royalty-free music libraries.
*   **Efficiency:** Can compose a new piece of music in seconds, drastically accelerating the scoring process.

## 6. Pricing, Limits, and Economic Model

*   **Freemium & Subscription Tiers:**
    *   **Free Plan:** Limited downloads (3/month), non-commercial use, attribution required. AIVA retains copyright.
    *   **Standard Plan (€15/month):** More downloads (15/month), limited monetization (YouTube, TikTok), no attribution required. AIVA retains copyright.
    *   **Pro Plan (€49/month):** 300 downloads/month, full monetization, **full copyright transfer to user**.
*   **Copyright Ownership:** A key differentiator. Pro users gain full, perpetual copyright ownership of their generated compositions.

## 7. Safety, Policy, and Governance

*   **Copyright Clarity:** AIVA's clear stance on copyright ownership (especially for Pro users) addresses a major concern in AI-generated content.
*   **Ethical Training:** Trained on classical and public domain works, minimizing potential copyright infringement concerns in its own training data.

## 8. Adoption, Ecosystem, and Real-World Usage

*   **Independent Filmmakers & Game Developers:** Popular for creating affordable, high-quality bespoke soundtracks.
*   **Advertising Agencies:** For background music in commercials and brand content.
*   **Content Creators:** YouTubers and podcasters use it for royalty-free music that matches their content's mood.

## 9. Competitive Landscape

*   **Primary Competitor:** **Soundraw / Boomy**.
    *   **Comparison:** AIVA excels in classical/cinematic. Soundraw is more general-purpose and customizable. Boomy focuses on song structure.
*   **Secondary Competitor:** **Suno / Udio**.
    *   **Comparison:** Suno/Udio excel in vocal-driven pop/hip-hop. AIVA focuses on instrumental scores.

## 10. Operational Risks

*   **Niche Focus:** While strong in its niche, it might not be the best tool for diverse genres like vocal pop or experimental electronic music.
*   **API Lack:** No public API, limiting programmatic integration into custom applications.
